Looking Toward the Future

The next step on my journey is to get my "A" license so that I can jump as a civilian. I need to attend a certification course to do so, which will cost around $2,000 - $3,000. I'm hoping that some of my jumps will count towards my license, but they may not.

I want to get my Tandem Instructor certification so that I can jump my friends and family out of planes myself and let them experience my world.

I have found a facility nearby that I think I will start my courses at. The facility is called Skydive Palatka. The place isn't far away, and they offer a cheaper "accelerated Freefall" course. The only thing the couse doen't provide is the required amount of jumps for an "A" licesne, but hopefully I can get my jumps to count.
